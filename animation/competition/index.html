The Fine Print

Terms & Conditions

By taking part in the Cat & Mouse Chomp Challenge, entrants agree to the following.

  1. Eligibility. The challenge is open to members of the Impactus Learning community. Entrants must be able to attend the AI Animation Masterclass online if they win.
  2. How to enter. Entry is free. Simply play Andrew's Cat & Mouse Chomp at the official game link. No purchase is necessary.
  3. Identifiable name required. Entrants must play using a name that Impactus Learning can clearly recognise and trace back to them within the community (e.g. real name or known community handle). Entries with anonymous, unclear or unidentifiable names may be disqualified, as the prize cannot be awarded if the winner cannot be identified.
  4. Competition period. The challenge runs until Saturday, 20 June 2026 at midnight (Singapore Time, UTC+8). Scores recorded after this time will not be counted.
  5. Winning. The prize is awarded to the entrant with the single highest score on the game leaderboard at the close of the competition period.
  6. The prize. One (1) free seat at an AI Animation Masterclass session (value $120 SGD). The prize is non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash or any other item.
  7. Google AI Pro requirement. A Google AI Pro subscription is required to attend and follow along in the course. If the winner does not have an active Google AI Pro subscription, a one-month free subscription will be gifted to enable participation. This gifted subscription is not redeemable for cash and is non-transferable. If the winner already has an existing Google AI Pro subscription, no compensation or alternative will be given in its place.
  8. Winner announcement. The winner will be announced on Sunday, 21 June 2026 and notified directly. The winner must respond to claim the prize; if no response is received within 7 days, an alternate winner may be selected.
  9. Fair play. The decision of Impactus Learning is final.
  10. Prize redemption. The free seat applies to a scheduled masterclass session and is subject to seat availability on the chosen date. Standard masterclass prerequisites apply (e.g. a Google AI Pro subscription is required to follow along live).
  11. General. Impactus Learning reserves the right to amend, suspend or cancel the challenge, or these terms, at any time without prior notice. Participation constitutes acceptance of these terms.

Questions about the challenge? WhatsApp Dr. Andrew Chew or reach out via impactuslearning.com.
