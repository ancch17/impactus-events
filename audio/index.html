TRK 04

Before you ask.

Do I need any music background?

None at all — not a note. If you can describe the song you want in a sentence, you can make it. The class is pitched for beginners through to intermediate; we start from zero, and if you've already dabbled you'll pick up the producer moves in hour two.

What do I need to bring?

A laptop with working speakers (built-in is fine), a Suno account (see the next question about going paid), a free ElevenLabs account, and the free Audacity app. Set-up instructions are sent after you register.

Do I need the paid version of Suno?

Recommended, not required. Most of the class is a live demo you can follow clearly on the free tier. A paid plan lets you build along in real time and unlock the hour-two features shown on screen: hum-and-extend and Personas.

Is this live or recorded?

Live on Zoom, 7.30–9.30pm Singapore time. It's a mass class — mostly demo so you can see every move, with a light hands-on stretch and time for questions as you go.

When is it, and how long?

One evening only — Thursday 30 July 2026, 7.30–9.30pm SGT (2 hours), live on Zoom. Reserve your spot on Luma.

What will I walk away with?

A clear, repeatable workflow for making finished songs — seen end to end and tried yourself — plus, if you build along, a song of your own. And if you're ever curious about releasing your music, you'll know exactly how that door opens (DistroKid, streaming royalties) — with zero pressure to walk through it.
